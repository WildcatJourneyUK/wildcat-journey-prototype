Wildcat Journey is a gamified platform designed to guide international students from their first moment of curiosity about the University of Kentucky all the way to becoming Wildcats.

For prospective international students, it transforms the admissions and transition process into a clear journey with levels, quests, experience points, and mentorship connections.

For international ambassadors, it provides a structured system to support and mentor students with similar interests, academic goals, languages, and backgrounds.

And for the International Center and Admissions Officers, it serves as a centralized space for curated marketing content, making outreach more strategic, relevant, and personalized.

# Overview

Wildcat Journey transforms the student journey into an interactive progression system inspired by game design.

Students move through levels such as:

- Explorer
- Applicant
- Admitted
- Current Wildcat

As they progress, they complete quests, earn XP, unlock badges, and connect with ambassadors who share similar backgrounds, languages, or academic interests.

The platform supports three different user roles:

- Students
- Ambassadors
- Admissions Officers

Each role has its own dashboard and tools designed to support engagement, mentorship, and outreach.

# Platform Features

## Student Dashboard

The student dashboard acts as the central hub of the experience.

Students can:

- Track their current level and XP
- View available quests
- Complete interactive checklists
- Watch short explainer videos
- Take mini quizzes
- Connect with matched ambassadors
- Unlock badges and rewards

Quests are progressively unlocked as students move through the application journey.

## Quest System

Each quest provides clear guidance for students navigating the admissions process.

Every quest includes:

- A clear explanation
- Step-by-step instructions
- Short explainer videos
- Mini quizzes
- Interactive checklists
- XP rewards

This structure turns complex processes into manageable actions.

## Ambassador Dashboard

Ambassadors can mentor prospective students and manage engagement.

Features include:

- Student mentorship matching
- Student profile overview
- Quest management
- Badge creation and management
- Opportunity management

Students are automatically matched with ambassadors based on:

- Country
- Language
- Intended major
- Interests
  
## Admissions Officer Dashboard

The admissions dashboard helps the admissions team manage outreach content more efficiently.

Admissions officers can:

- Filter video content by continent and region
- Access international student stories
- Share targeted content during outreach events
- Support ambassadors and students through the platform

# Security Model

To ensure that only official team members access administrative features, role upgrades require an **invite code verification system**.

**Roles include:** Student (default), Ambassador, and Admissions Officer

Ambassadors and admissions officers must enter a valid invite code to unlock their dashboards.

# Tech Stack

- **Frontend:** ``React`` ``TypeScript`` ``TailwindCSS`` ``React Router``   
- **Backend:** ``Supabase Authentication`` ``Supabase Database``    
- **Other:** ``YouTube embeds for video content`` ``Role-based dashboard routing``    

# Project Vision

Wildcat Journey aims to make the international student journey more structured and easier to navigate.

By combining technology, mentorship, and gamified progression, the platform helps students understand the steps ahead, connect with ambassadors with similar backgrounds, and discover opportunities that match their interests.

# Status

This repository contains a **prototype** developed as part of a proposal for the University of Kentucky International Ambassador Program.

The prototype demonstrates the core user experience, authentication, platform structure, and dashboard concepts, but most features are currently mocked or simplified.
